# 🎲 Module Template — running a scenario as a session
> [!info] The reusable wrapper that turns any [[Bestiary Index|bestiary entry]] into a runnable exercise. The flagship instance is the [[ISF 2026 SPICE Workshop (external kit)]].

**1. Frame** — pick the perimeter & P&L ([[Canon & Figures Register]]); designate a Finance Controller ([[Marc Lindqvist (Finance Controller)|Marc's role]]).
**2. Red-team (15')** — sponsor → business objective → one path. Stop over-engineering.
**3. Pivot** — reveal the chosen bestiary entry's one-paragraph path.
**4. Consequence timeline (20')** — service recovery ≠ financial recovery.
**5. Financial impact (15')** — bracket, don't compute; one headline number.
**6. Wrap** — harvest; reveal the worked case; the dual-state payoff.

**Inputs:** a bestiary entry · the [[Elicitation Prompt Tables]] · the external GM kit (runbook, cheat-sheet, quant pack).
**Output:** a priced `SpiceScenario` (3 cases) ready for the [[Data Dictionary|graph]].
