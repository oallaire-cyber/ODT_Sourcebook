# 🧑‍💼 Henrik Sorensen
**Role:** VP Program Management (Program Director, HORIZON-LEO & AURORA-GEO) · **Reports to:** [[Elena Vasquez (CEO)]]

> [!quote] In character
> "Everyone in this room owns a risk. I own the *date*. Whatever you bring me — a supplier breach, a launch slip, a regulator's letter — my first question is the same: what does it do to the critical path?"

## Mandate
**End-to-end accountability for HORIZON-LEO and [[Annex - AURORA-GEO Programme|AURORA-GEO]] delivery** — schedule, budget and integration across Engineering, Manufacturing, Launch and Ground. The single throat to choke for *"is the constellation on time?"* The functional VPs ([[Carlos Mendes (VP Manufacturing & Supply Chain)|Carlos]], [[Yuki Tanaka (VP Launch Operations)|Yuki]], [[Priya Nair (VP Engineering)|Priya]], [[Sofia Adler (VP Ground & Operations)|Sofia]]) own their risks operationally; Henrik owns the **portfolio critical path** that ties them together. He directly holds the AURORA-GEO programme as its Program Director through PDR.

## Objectives owned
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-02]] — Phase 3 deployment by Q2 2027.
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-05]] — AURORA-GEO pass PDR gate by Q1 2027.

## What keeps them up at night
A **Phase-4 slip** that quietly becomes a missed government coverage promise. AURORA's **optical ISL not maturing** in time for PDR. A strategic partner walking out mid-programme. He is the one who reads a supplier intrusion ([[BST-SC1 Supplier Build-Chain Intrusion (Supply Chain)|SC1]]) as a *schedule* event weeks before it's filed as a *security* one.

## Risk footprint (graph)
- **`BEARS`**: RA-01 (PDR technical readiness delay), RA-04 (strategic partner withdrawal) — held in his AURORA Program Director capacity. Portfolio-accountable (escalation owner) for RH-01 (Phase 3 schedule, operationally Yuki's) and RH-02 (on-orbit performance, Priya's).
- **`STEWARDS`**: programme-level schedule and budget controls (buffers, EVM, milestone gates); MIT-09 (AURORA optical-link risk-reduction campaign) runs in his programme under the AURORA Chief Engineer.
- **Bestiary**: the schedule consequence of [[BST-SC1 Supplier Build-Chain Intrusion (Supply Chain)|SC1]] (Phase-4 slip) and [[BST-GP1 Export-Control & Launch Disruption (Geopolitical)|GP1]] (launch slip) both land on his programme before they reach Finance.

## Their language
Critical path, PDR gate, milestone, EVM, buffer, integration, work-package. Translates *everything* — attack, supplier, regulator — into *"what does this do to the date?"*

## Appears in
