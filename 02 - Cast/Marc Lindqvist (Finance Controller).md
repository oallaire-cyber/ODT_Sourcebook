# 🧑‍🚀 Marc Lindqvist
**Role:** Finance Controller · **Reports to:** [[Diane Marchetti (CFO)]]

> [!quote] In the serial
> *"He wasn't building a model. He was translating."* The collaborator Tom expected to be an adversary.

## Mandate
Runs the controlling function that prices SPICE timelines against the real operating plan. Margin data stays inside Finance — which is exactly what makes Finance willing to participate. He owns the *pricing* leg of triple validation.

## Objectives owned
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-01]] hold the AOP
- defensible order-of-magnitude figures

## What keeps them up at night
False precision. *"If I tell the board this costs €68.4M, they'll argue about the point-four and miss the sixty-eight."* Double-counting. Banking insurance that won't pay.
