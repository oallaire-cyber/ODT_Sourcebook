# 🧑‍🚀 Sofia Adler
**Role:** VP Ground & Operations · **Reports to:** [[Elena Vasquez (CEO)]] (via COO)

> [!quote] In the serial
> She runs the ground stations and the two operations centres; she's the one who names the consequence before anyone names the attack.

## Mandate
End-to-end accountability for the **ground segment** — 4 gateways + 12 secondary stations, the **Denver primary NOC and Dublin backup**, mission-control and network-management software, 24/7 service delivery. She owns *service recovery*; she is first to feel the gap between service recovery and **financial** recovery.

## Objectives owned
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-01]] EBITDA via service uptime
- 99.5% space-segment availability (SLAs)

## What keeps them up at night
The constellation in **fleet-wide safe-mode** on a Monday morning — *"nobody's getting service, and we keep paying for everything anyway."* The shared Denver/Dublin identity plane: *"whoever locked us out of one is already inside the other."*

Operational and concrete: dashboards, safe-mode, gateways, control-plane recovery, recertification time. Translates 'attack' into 'what stops working'.
