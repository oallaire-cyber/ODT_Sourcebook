# 🧑‍🚀 Diane Marchetti
**Role:** CFO (external hire 2026, ex-public-company) · **Reports to:** [[Elena Vasquez (CEO)]]

> [!quote] In the serial
> *"Allergic to hand-waving... and then she did something Tom had never seen a CFO do with a security topic. She got interested."*

## Mandate
Owns the AOP, the IPO file and the insurance programme. She is the consumer of the SPICE number — the proof that quantified-consequence beats guessed-probability is that **she acts on it** (and asks to do it again, with a different risk — the on-ramp to [[Bestiary Index|the bestiary expansion]]).

## Objectives owned
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TCO-01]] sustain EBITDA
- a clean, defensible IPO risk disclosure

## What keeps them up at night
An analyst discovering an outage scenario ODT never priced. A cyber 'colour' or 'score out of five' she can't use. Insurance cover that is *a fiction* because it excludes the most likely attacker.

## Risk footprint (graph)
- **`BEARS`**: [[Canon & Figures Register|RC-01]] EBITDA, RC-02 runway, RC-03 investor confidence
- **`STEWARDS`**: insurance programme, IPO file, the AOP

## Their language
Decisions, in her units: insurance placement, investment trade-offs (*'reduces a €69M exposure by X'*), the IPO file. *"The first cyber risk anyone has brought me that I can actually use."*
