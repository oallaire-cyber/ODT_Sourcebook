# 🧑‍🚀 Yuki Tanaka
**Role:** VP Launch Operations · **Reports to:** [[Elena Vasquez (CEO)]] (via COO); programme dotted-line to [[Henrik Sorensen (Program Director)]]

> [!quote] In character
> "I don't control the rockets and I don't control the debris field. I control *options* — three launchers and a manoeuvre budget. Take either away and the manifest is a wish, not a plan."

## Mandate
Owns **launcher coordination, range access, orbital mechanics, TLE management and deployment manoeuvres** — the committed 2025–2029 multi-launcher manifest (SpaceX Falcon 9, Rocket Lab Electron, future Axiom). She gets [[Carlos Mendes (VP Manufacturing & Supply Chain)|Carlos]]'s satellites to orbit and hands a working fleet to [[Sofia Adler (VP Ground & Operations)|Sofia]].

## Objectives owned
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-02]] — complete Phase 3 deployment by Q2 2027.
- [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-06]] — maintain zero debris-generation incidents.

## What keeps them up at night
**Manifest congestion** opening a 6+ month launch gap right before a government coverage promise. And the one-shot reputational event: *"one collision during a deployment burn and we're the company that littered LEO — that's not a schedule problem, that's the IPO."* Export control freezing an allied launcher she was counting on.

- **`BEARS`**: RH-01 (Phase 3 launch schedule delay — business), ROL-01 (launch vehicle availability gap), ROL-02 (orbital debris collision during deployment).
- **`STEWARDS`**: MIT-01 (multi-launcher diversification strategy), MIT-07 (autonomous collision avoidance system).
- **Bestiary**: her ROL-01 is a named cause of [[BST-GP1 Export-Control & Launch Disruption (Geopolitical)|GP1]]; ROL-02 propagates to RC-05 (reputational damage).

Manifest, slip, conjunction, delta-v, range, rideshare, insertion, decay. Translates 'geopolitics' into *"do I still have a ride, and is the orbit still clear?"*
