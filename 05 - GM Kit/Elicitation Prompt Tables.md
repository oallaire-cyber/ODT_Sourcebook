# 🎛️ Elicitation Prompt Tables (GM-only)
> [!info] The questions a facilitator rolls through in a **real** risk-owner interview (slower than the timed canvas). Pair with [[Cast Roster]] — ask each character their own column.

## Objectives (→ TCO/TPO)
- "What must be true at year-end for this to count as a good year?" → [[Canon & Figures Register|TCO]] candidates.
- "Which programme milestones gate the next funding event?" → TPO candidates.

## Risk by perimeter (don't lead)
- "Monday morning, first coffee — what's the worst thing waiting?" (Maya's opener)
- "What keeps running and costing money even when the service stops?"

## Scenario, three cases (→ SpiceScenario)
- "Best / realistic / pessimistic — give me three, not one."
- "When is **service** nominal, and when are the **finances** nominal?" (the recovery gap)

## Influence edges (→ INFLUENCES)
- "If this fails, what fails next? How sure are you — weak, moderate, strong, critical?"
- "Does anything in another team's world make this *more* likely?" (surfaces cross-family edges → [[Influence Map]])

## Finance translation (→ EBIT/FCF)
- "Don't compute — bracket it. 5% or 25%?" · "Are we double-counting revenue **and** the penalty?"

> [!tip] The interconnection prompt
> Always ask the cross-domain question. It is the one that turns a [[Bestiary Index|register of monsters]] into a graph.
