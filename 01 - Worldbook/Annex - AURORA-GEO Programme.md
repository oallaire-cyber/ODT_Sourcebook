# 🛰️ Annex — AURORA-GEO Programme (full second context)
> [!info] The second P&L perimeter alongside HORIZON-LEO. Gives RIM its portfolio / cross-perimeter story.

## What it is
**AURORA-GEO** — an early-development **GEO relay satellite** with **optical inter-satellite links (ISL)**, **co-funded by ESA/NASA**. Provides high-capacity backhaul/relay for the LEO fleet and third-party missions. Currently **Phase A/B pre-development**, PDR targeted **Q1 2027** (objective [[Canon & Figures Register|TPO-05]]).

## Why it matters to the risk story
- A **different risk profile** from HORIZON-LEO: technology-maturity (TRL) and **funding-milestone** risk dominate, not service/SLA risk. The recovery metric is *programme schedule and co-funding*, not EBIT.
- It is a **diversification bet** for [[Canon & Figures Register|TCO-03]] — and therefore exposed to the same financing crunch as the constellation. This is a cross-perimeter influence into [[BST-FN1 IPO Window Collapse (Financial)|FN1]].

## Its risks (graph)
`RA-01..RA-05` — incl. **RA-02 Development budget overrun**, strategic-partner withdrawal, PDR slip, TRL shortfall, ESA/NASA funding commitment.
